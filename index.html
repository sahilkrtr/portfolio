Biography

Born and raised in India, Sahil Tripathi is currently a PhD student at Jamia Hamdard, where he is passionate about using cutting-edge technologies to solve real-world challenges. His research focuses on Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning (DL), and Natural Language Processing (NLP). Sahil is particularly interested in exploring ways to align AI systems with human values to make them more explainable, robust, and socially beneficial. His work bridges his ongoing interests in ML applications and AI ethics, aiming to contribute to practical AI adoption in critical fields.

Sahil has always demonstrated a strong inclination toward learning and self-improvement. His problem-solving mindset and creative approach drive him to seek innovative solutions to complex challenges, particularly in AI and optimization. He earned his Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications (BCA) and Master's degree in Computer Applications (MCA) from Jamia Hamdard, where he was committed to academic excellence. His skills include Python, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Data Structures & Algorithms, SQL, Core Java, DBMS, OOPs, and Problem Solving. With a passion for research, Sahil's academic journey continues to reflect his dedication to advancing AI and its practical applications.
